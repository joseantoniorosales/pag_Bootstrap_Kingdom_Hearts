Welcome to Kingdom Hearts, a fantasy universe home of both characters from Final Fantasy and Disney. Join our heroes Sora, Donald and Goofy in their adventure in order to finish once and for all with the darkness using the legendary power of the Keyblade

Origins

The idea of combining Disney characters with the Final Fantasy atmosphere ocurred while SquareSoft and Disney's headquarter were shared by the two companies.

One day, Tetsuya Nomura (director and character designer of the game) and an important employee of Disney were discussing about this idea. It took a while to be accepted but, after a few rejections, Kingdom Hearts was born.

Story I

The story of the saga is considered as one of the hardest to follow, but, in fact, is not that hard, just... too fragmented.

The story is like this (this is a short version just with the main points and stopping at the most understandable parts): A long time ago the world was almost engulfed by darkness in the keyblade war, but the remnants of light could isolate different worlds from the darkness. Several years later, the keyblade master known as Xehanort wants to recreate this event to achieve a legendary realm that can grant any wish: The Kingdom Hearts, but he was stopped by Terra, Aqua and Ventus, main characters from Birth by Sleep.

Story II

After the plans of Xehanort were frustated, he ends up possesing Terra's bodies and dividing himself into a heartless (Ansem the Seeker of Darkness) and a Nobody (Xemnas).

10 years of peace has passed, and we start to follow Sora, a young teenager that spends his days playing with is friends Riku and Kairi in the Destiny Islands, until one day the darkness destroys his world. Suddenly, a voice start talking to him and gives him the right to wield the legendary Keyblade. As the franchise continues you will start to understand what's happening and if not... You can always rely on the wikis.

Kingdom Hearts

Where the legend of the keyblade began. Join the young Sora on his adventure to erradicate the darkness within the godly realm where hearts inhabit: The Kingdom Hearts

Kingdom Hearts II

The second numbered tittle. A year after the events of the first game, Sora has to face once again the forces of the heartless and a new former enemy: the Organization XIII

Kingdom Hearts III

The last installment of the franchise. The final battle against the true Organization XIII has just started. But will the light shine again or will it fade away for the rest of eternity...?

Development

The games normally have been developed normally in 1-3 years, but with Kingdom Hearts III It took almost 12 years to finish It (This happened because there were some problems between Disney and Pixar to get the rights of some of their movies for the game), but the rest of them were released normally in the mid-term.

Opinion

Kingdom Hearts is a franchise that has set the standar of the modern action-RPG games and has grown bigger and bigger since the first installment of the franchise was release, firstly for Disney and later for Its incredible combat system, a combination of real time action and the commands of classic final fantasy games.

For me, Kingdom Hearts II is my favourite game of all times because of his extremely fast-paced combat system and the execution of all the scenery.
